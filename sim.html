Tournament Rules
⏱️
Game Length
6 innings or 2-hour time limit for 8U–12U. No new inning may start after the 2:00 mark. Pool play games may end in a tie. Championship games have no time limit.
🏆
Seeding Criteria
1. Record · 2. Runs Against · 3. Runs Scored · 4. Head to Head · 5. Coin Flip. Top 8 teams advance to Sunday bracket.
🔁
Extra Innings
International tie-break rules in elimination games. Last recorded out placed on second base with 1 out. Used only when a winner must advance.
🚨
Mercy Rule
12 runs after 3 innings · 10 runs after 4 innings · 8 runs after 5 innings. If the home team reaches the mercy threshold, the game ends immediately.
👟
Field & Equipment
50–70 foot dimensions. Rubber cleats. Age cutoff is April 30th. Bats must display BPF 1.15, BBCOR, or USA Baseball designation.
🏃
Roster & Lineup
Bat 9 or more players. Any player on roster may play any defensive position. Courtesy runner allowed for pitcher or catcher at any time — must be a player not in the lineup.
⚠️
Balks (11U)
One warning per pitcher at the 11U level. Balk warnings reset between pitchers. Sportsmanship is emphasized — ejections result in a one-game tournament suspension.
🌧️
Rain Policy
0 games played = Full Refund. 1 game played = Partial Refund. 2 games played = No Refund. Tournament will make every effort to complete play.
