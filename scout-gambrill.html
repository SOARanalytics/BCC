Coaching keys
Game plan
Pitching vs Gambrill
  • Do not walk anyone. Every free pass becomes a stolen base. The running game ignites off walks more than anything.
  • Work the corners. They're a contact team — weak contact to the right spots beats Ks. Groundball outs are your friend.
  • Watch for delayed steals on any deep count or passed ball. Noah J, Matias K, and Johnny C have all stolen home.
  • Free outs: Connor K (#8), Cooper T (#12), Harry E (#16), Patrick M (#14) — attack the zone early vs all four.
Hitting vs Gambrill
  • vs Brayden G or Oliver W: Both get ahead early — look for first-pitch strikes to drive. Don't let them get ahead 0-2.
  • vs Parker W, Ty J, Kaden T, Hudson K: Take first pitch — all below 50% FPS. Work the walk. They'll fall behind.
  • Put ball in play. Gambrill arms give up contact hits regularly. Be aggressive when ahead in the count.
  • Cohen B, Ty J — if they enter, they're walk dispensers. Take pitches and let them work themselves out of the zone.
