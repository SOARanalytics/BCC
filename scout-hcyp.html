Coaching keys
Game plan
Pitching vs HCYP
  • No walks. Every base on balls leads directly to a steal. Their entire lineup runs, and their catcher allows it. One walk = runner on second in 2 pitches.
  • King A (#26), Colin S (#17), Daniel E (#24) get nothing fat. Work corners, change speeds, pitch backwards. These three can beat you alone.
  • Elevate to Kellan B (#11). He has 13 popup outs — go up in the zone and let him put it up. Easiest out on the team.
  • Attack Mason W (#1) in the zone. 38% strikeout rate — he's the most vulnerable bat in the lineup. Don't nibble — throw strikes.
  • Slide-step from the stretch. Daniel E (44 SB), Gio L (29), Brennan B (25) will run at first pitch. Quick delivery limits their jump.
  • Calvin P (#35) is extreme pull. Shade your LF/3B toward the line. 29 balls to LF vs 7 to RF this season.
Hitting vs HCYP
  • vs Mason W and Mason B: Both work ahead — look for first-pitch strikes to drive. Their best ERA pitchers get ahead 0-1 quickly. Don't let them get to 0-2.
  • vs King A, Dominic S, Luke C: All three have high walk rates (BB/7 above 6.5). Take pitches, work the count, force them to throw strikes in the zone.
  • vs Calvin P: 15.4 K/7 — the most swing-and-miss stuff on the staff. Be selective. His walk rate is also high, so patience pays.
  • String at-bats together. HCYP pitching is hittable when they fall behind. Avoid the big K inning by keeping at-bats alive with foul balls and two-strike approach.
  • Their FPS% is 54–62% across all arms — they work ahead. Expect first-pitch strikes from most pitchers. Be ready to swing early.
