Coaching focus
Game plan
Pitching keys
  • No walks — period. Every walk becomes a stolen base in 2 pitches. 13% CS rate means runners go and aren't getting caught. A walk is effectively a double.
  • Slots 1–4 get nothing fat. Daniel E, Kellan B, Colin S, King A. Work corners, change eye level, pitch backwards. These four carry the lineup.
  • Elevate to Kellan B (#11). 13 popup outs this season — highest rate on team. Go up in the zone and let him put it up.
  • Attack Mason W (#1) in the zone. 38% K rate — the only real vulnerability in this lineup. Don't nibble. Throw strikes and let him beat himself.
  • Slide-step every pitch from the stretch. Daniel E (44 SB), Gio L (29 SB), Brennan B (25 SB) all run at first pitch. Quick delivery is mandatory.
  • Calvin P (#35) — shift LF hard. 29 balls to LF, only 6 to RF. Pull hitter. Move your LF and 3B toward the line.
Hitting keys
  • vs Mason W and Mason B: Both get ahead early (59–61% FPS). Look for first-pitch strikes to drive — be ready to swing early in the count.
  • vs King A, Dominic S, Luke C: All have BB/7 above 6.5. Take borderline pitches, work counts, force them into the zone. Patience produces walks and traffic.
  • vs Calvin P: 15.4 K/7 is most swing-and-miss stuff on staff. Be selective — but his walk rate is high. Discipline will get you on base.
  • We can run on their catcher. 13% CS rate allowed. Our runners should be aggressive after walks and 2-strike counts. Attack their arm all game.
  • The window: HCYP allows 7+ runs in 28% of games. String at-bats in innings 3–4. Make contact, stack runners, and let their pitching depth break down.
